# Character Abilities & Skill Trees

## Ability System Overview

Each character has:
- **Basic Abilities**: Unlocked through leveling (5-7 abilities per character)
- **Skill Tree Abilities**: Unlocked by spending AP in skill trees
- **Resonance Limit Break**: Ultimate ability unlocked at high bond level
- **Equipment Abilities**: Granted by specific weapons/armor

---

## KAEL VOSS - The Echo Bridge

**Role**: Balanced All-Rounder | **Weapon Types**: Swords, Daggers

### Basic Abilities (Learned by Level)

**Echo Strike** (Lv. 1)
- Cost: 15 EE
- Physical attack that captures enemy's essence, slightly stronger against Echoes
- Can steal buffs from enemies

**Resonance Pulse** (Lv. 5)
- Cost: 25 EE, 30% Resonance
- AoE magic damage, hits all enemies
- Damage increases with Resonance gauge

**Memory Weave** (Lv. 10)
- Cost: 20 EE
- Buff: Increases party's Resonance generation for 3 turns
- Also slightly heals party

**Adaptive Strike** (Lv. 18)
- Cost: 30 EE
- Physical attack that changes element based on enemy weakness
- Requires previous turn analysis

**Echo Fusion** (Lv. 25)
- Cost: 40 EE, 50% Resonance
- Temporarily combine two slotted Echoes for unique effect
- Effects discovered through experimentation

**Reality Anchor** (Lv. 35)
- Cost: 35 EE
- Support: Remove all debuffs from party, prevent time/reality manipulation for 2 turns
- Essential for late-game enemies

**Convergence Touch** (Lv. 45)
- Cost: 60 EE, 75% Resonance
- Massive single-target damage
- Heals Kael for percentage of damage dealt
- Risk: 25% chance to enter Overload

### Skill Trees

**HUNTER PATH** (Focus: Echo Collection & Versatility)

Tier 1:
- **Echo Sensitivity**: +25% chance to find rare Echoes
- **Quick Capture**: Using Echo abilities costs 10% less EE

Tier 2:
- **Echo Mastery**: Can equip 7 Echoes instead of 6
- **Adaptive Resonance**: Can use any Echo type at +20% effectiveness

Tier 3:
- **Perfect Harmony**: Echo abilities can't be reflected or countered
- **CAPSTONE - Infinite Echo**: Once per battle, use any collected Echo even if not equipped

---

**MEMORY PATH** (Focus: Magic & Resonance)

Tier 1:
- **Memory Bank**: +30% max EE
- **Resonance Overflow**: Can exceed 100% Resonance gauge (up to 150%)

Tier 2:
- **Crystalline Mind**: Resistance to mental status effects (confuse, charm, fear)
- **Echo Amplification**: All magic damage +25%

Tier 3:
- **Memory Storm**: New Ability - Spend all Echoes at once for massive AoE damage
- **CAPSTONE - Perfect Recall**: Can reuse expended Echoes once per battle

---

**BRIDGE PATH** (Focus: Party Support & Balance)

Tier 1:
- **Empathic Bond**: Passively share Resonance with allies
- **Stabilizing Presence**: Allies near Kael have +10% all stats

Tier 2:
- **Resonance Transfer**: Can give Resonance gauge % to allies
- **Echo Share**: Used Echoes can be triggered again by allies

Tier 3:
- **Reality Weaver**: New Ability - Manipulate turn order, push/pull ally or enemy turns
- **CAPSTONE - Between Worlds**: Kael exists slightly out of phase, 30% dodge chance, can move through enemies

---

### Limit Break

**FRAGMENTS OF INFINITY**
- Requires: 100% Resonance, S-rank bond unlocked
- Effect: Kael splits into multiple versions across possible timelines
- Creates 3 temporary Kael copies that each take a turn
- After all copies act, they merge back with AoE damage
- Kael's Resonance resets to 0%
- Cannot be used while Hollow

## IRIS MANTLE - The Iron Engineer

**Role**: Physical DPS / Debuffer | **Weapon Types**: Hammers, Rifles

### Basic Abilities

**Heavy Strike** (Lv. 3)
- Cost: 18 EE
- Powerful single-target physical attack
- 30% chance to stun

**EMP Burst** (Lv. 7)
- Cost: 25 EE
- Disables mechanical enemies, heavily damages constructs
- Removes buffs from affected targets

**Overclock** (Lv. 12)
- Cost: 30 EE
- Self-buff: +50% attack speed for 3 turns, +30% damage
- Drawback: Take 10% max HP damage per turn

**Shrapnel Shot** (Lv. 20)
- Cost: 35 EE
- Ranged attack that hits random enemies 5 times
- Can hit same enemy multiple times

**Prototype Deploy** (Lv. 28)
- Cost: 40 EE, 40% Resonance
- Summon combat drone that fights alongside party for 5 turns
- Drone gets more powerful based on skill tree choices

**Resonance Disruptor** (Lv. 38)
- Cost: 50 EE
- Debuff all enemies: -50% Resonance generation for 3 turns
- Enemies can't enter Resonant state

**Railgun** (Lv. 48)
- Cost: 70 EE, 60% Resonance
- Extreme single-target piercing damage
- Ignores defense, always critical vs mechanical enemies

### Skill Trees

**ARSENAL PATH** (Focus: Raw Damage)

Tier 1:
- **Weapon Specialist**: +20% damage with equipped weapon type
- **Heavy Hitter**: Critical hits deal 2.5x damage instead of 2x

Tier 2:
- **Armor Piercer**: Ignore 30% of enemy Defense
- **Explosive Rounds**: All attacks have small AoE splash damage

Tier 3:
- **Devastation**: New Ability - Massive damage that destroys part of environment, can reveal secrets
- **CAPSTONE - One Shot**: Once per battle, guarantee critical hit with 3x damage

---

**ENGINEERING PATH** (Focus: Gadgets & Debuffs)

Tier 1:
- **Field Repairs**: Can use items on allies without consuming turn
- **Efficient Design**: All gadget abilities cost 20% less EE

Tier 2:
- **Tactical Analysis**: Can scan enemies to reveal stats and weaknesses
- **Prototype Upgrade**: Deployed drones have +50% stats and last 2 turns longer

Tier 3:
- **Sabotage**: New Ability - Permanently reduce enemy attack/defense by 20%
- **CAPSTONE - Master Engineer**: Can deploy two drones simultaneously

---

**CYBERNETICS PATH** (Focus: Prosthetic Arm Abilities)

Tier 1:
- **Reinforced Plating**: +25% Defense, Iris takes less damage from physical attacks
- **Power Arm**: Cybernetic arm attacks deal bonus damage

Tier 2:
- **Grapple Hook**: Can pull enemies out of turn order or pull self to safety
- **Energy Blade**: Cybernetic arm can deal physical or magic damage

Tier 3:
- **Overcharge**: New Ability - Transform arm into energy cannon for 3 turns, massive power boost
- **CAPSTONE - Full Integration**: Iris can't be disarmed or have equipment disabled, immune to tech-based debuffs

---

### Limit Break

**PHOENIX PROTOCOL**
- Requires: 100% Resonance, S-rank bond unlocked
- Effect: Iris's cybernetics fully activate, granting extreme combat mode
- +100% all stats for 5 turns
- All attacks hit entire enemy party
- Immune to all status effects
- After duration, Resonance sets to 0% and Iris is exhausted (-50% stats for 2 turns)

## GARRICK THORNE - The Shield of Ash

**Role**: Tank / Support | **Weapon Types**: Shields, Maces

### Basic Abilities

**Guardian's Stand** (Lv. 2)
- Cost: 20 EE
- Taunt: All enemies target Garrick for 2 turns
- Garrick takes 30% less damage during duration

**Purifying Light** (Lv. 6)
- Cost: 25 EE
- Remove negative status effects from party
- Small heal to affected targets

**Shield Bash** (Lv. 11)
- Cost: 22 EE
- Physical attack that pushes enemy back in turn order
- Moderate damage with high stun chance

**Martyr's Resolve** (Lv. 19)
- Cost: 35 EE, 30% Resonance
- Redirect all damage to party to Garrick for 2 turns
- Garrick gains temporary HP shield

**Crystal Purge** (Lv. 27)
- Cost: 40 EE
- Cleanse area of Resonance corruption
- Damages Echo-type enemies heavily, heals party

**Unbreakable** (Lv. 37)
- Cost: 50 EE, 50% Resonance
- Garrick becomes immune to damage for 1 turn
- Can still act normally
- Any damage prevented is stored and released as counter-attack

**Last Stand** (Lv. 46)
- Passive
- If Garrick would be KO'd, survive at 1 HP once per battle
- Gain +100% Defense for 3 turns

### Skill Trees

**FORTRESS PATH** (Focus: Defense & Protection)

Tier 1:
- **Immovable**: Garrick can't be knocked back or moved
- **Shield Mastery**: +30% Defense when equipped with shield

Tier 2:
- **Protect**: Can cover adjacent allies, taking hits for them
- **Reinforced**: +500 max HP

Tier 3:
- **Bulwark**: New Ability - Create barrier that blocks all damage to party for 1 turn
- **CAPSTONE - Living Wall**: Garrick's presence reduces all party damage by 20% passively

---

**REDEMPTION PATH** (Focus: Support & Healing)

Tier 1:
- **Healing Hands**: Basic healing ability unlocked (30 EE, moderate heal)
- **Atonement**: Heals are 30% more effective

Tier 2:
- **Protective Aura**: Allies near Garrick regenerate HP slowly
- **Second Chance**: Can revive fallen ally at 50% HP (60 EE, once per battle)

Tier 3:
- **Sanctuary**: New Ability - Create safe zone that prevents all status effects for 3 turns
- **CAPSTONE - Redemption's Light**: Revive all fallen allies at full HP (once per battle, very expensive)

---

**JUDGMENT PATH** (Focus: Offensive Support)

Tier 1:
- **Righteous Fury**: Deal 50% more damage to "evil" enemy types
- **Hammer of Justice**: Mace attacks can deal holy damage

Tier 2:
- **Condemn**: Mark enemy to take increased damage from all sources
- **Holy Fire**: Attacks have chance to burn enemies

Tier 3:
- **Divine Judgment**: New Ability - Massive damage to single target based on damage they've dealt
- **CAPSTONE - Paladin's Wrath**: Can attack and defend in same turn

---

### Limit Break

**SHIELD OF THE FALLEN**
- Requires: 100% Resonance, S-rank bond unlocked
- Effect: Garrick channels the memories of all those he failed to save
- Become immortal for 5 turns (cannot be reduced below 1 HP)
- All party members gain +50% Defense
- Garrick's attacks heal party for percentage of damage dealt
- Ends with full party heal

## NYX - The Hollows Child

**Role**: Wild Card Mage | **Weapon Types**: Staves, Orbs

### Basic Abilities

**Void Bolt** (Lv. 4)
- Cost: 20 EE
- Magic damage, ignores resistance
- Damage varies wildly (50% to 150% of expected)

**Phase Shift** (Lv. 8)
- Cost: 30 EE
- Become untargetable for 1 turn
- Next attack deals double damage

**Reality Break** (Lv. 14)
- Cost: 35 EE, 40% Resonance
- Random powerful effect:
  - Massive damage to all enemies
  - Full heal to party
  - Swap HP/EE values
  - Shuffle turn order completely
  - Other strange effects

**Shadow Bind** (Lv. 22)
- Cost: 40 EE
- Immobilize all enemies for 1 turn
- Enemies take damage if they try to act

**Emotional Storm** (Lv. 30)
- Cost: 55 EE, 60% Resonance
- Based on Nyx's current "emotion"
  - Happy: Massive heal + buffs
  - Sad: Debuff enemies severely
  - Angry: Extreme AoE damage
  - Scared: Party becomes evasive
  - Curious: Random enemy analysis + weakness exploit

**Existence Flux** (Lv. 40)
- Cost: 65 EE
- Nyx and target enemy swap states
- Enemy becomes ally, Nyx becomes "enemy" (controlled as enemy AI)
- Lasts until Nyx-enemy is "defeated" then they swap back

**Un-Being** (Lv. 50)
- Cost: 80 EE, 90% Resonance
- Remove one enemy from reality for 5 turns
- Enemy returns confused and weakened

### Skill Trees

**CHAOS PATH** (Focus: Unpredictability & Power)

Tier 1:
- **Embrace Chaos**: Reality Break effects are more extreme
- **Wild Magic**: All abilities have 20% chance to cast twice

Tier 2:
- **Entropy**: Enemies near Nyx lose buffs over time
- **Unpredictable**: Nyx gains random stat boost each turn

Tier 3:
- **Chaos Storm**: New Ability - Everyone (including allies) takes random effects
- **CAPSTONE - Pure Chaos**: Once per battle, trigger all of Nyx's random effects simultaneously

---

**IDENTITY PATH** (Focus: Self-Discovery & Consistency)

Tier 1:
- **Finding Self**: Nyx's damage becomes more consistent (less variance)
- **Emotional Control**: Can choose Emotional Storm effect instead of random

Tier 2:
- **Personal Reality**: Nyx immune to reality-warping effects
- **Unique Existence**: +40% damage against Echo and Aberration enemies

Tier 3:
- **I Am Real**: New Ability - Prove existence, dealing damage based on Nyx's belief in self
- **CAPSTONE - Defined Form**: Nyx no longer has random variance, gains consistent powerful abilities

---

**HOLLOWS PATH** (Focus: Reality Manipulation)

Tier 1:
- **Phase Master**: Phase Shift costs 50% less, lasts 2 turns
- **Between Worlds**: Nyx partially exists in multiple realities, +30% evasion

Tier 2:
- **Time Skip**: Can delay enemy turns
- **Spatial Tear**: Teleport anywhere on battlefield, create hazard zones

Tier 3:
- **Reality Weaver**: New Ability - Rewrite battle conditions (change terrain, remove hazards, etc.)
- **CAPSTONE - Hollows Heart**: Nyx transforms battlefield into mini-Hollows, all rules become flexible

---

## Design Notes

**Balance Philosophy**:
- Every character has unique role but can be built flexibly
- No "required" characters for any content
- Skill trees allow specialization without forcing it
- Late-game builds can become highly specialized or remain generalized

**Progression Feel**:
- Start with basic abilities
- Gradually unlock more complex options
- Skill trees provide meaningful choices
- Limit Breaks are earned through story and relationships

**Synergies**:
- Characters combo well together (Kael + anyone)
- Some abilities specifically synergize (Iris's debuffs + Cipher's speed)
- Party composition matters but isn't rigid
- Experimentation is rewarded
