# Cultural & World Details

## Daily Life in Aethermoor

### Currency & Economy

**Resonance Credits (RC)**
- Standard currency across all regions
- Backed by purified Resonance crystal fragments
- Physical: Crystal chips of standardized sizes
- Digital: Stored in personal Resonance devices (for those who trust them)

**Typical Prices**:
- Loaf of bread: 5 RC
- Night at inn: 20-50 RC (depending on quality)
- Basic weapon: 100-500 RC
- Healing potion: 25 RC
- Rare Echo: 1000+ RC
- House: 10,000-50,000 RC

**Economic Disparity**:
- Ironcoast Federation: Wealthy, industrialized
- Crystalline Steppes: Middle class, trading economy
- Cindral Wastes: Poor, resource-scarce
- Verdant Tangle: Subsistence, barter-heavy
- The Hollows: No economy (uninhabitable)

### Food & Cuisine

#### Regional Specialties

**Cindral Wastes**:
- **Ash Bread**: Dark, dense bread baked in volcanic heat
- **Ember Fruit**: Spicy fruit that grows in hot soil
- **Magma Stew**: Extremely spicy meat and vegetable stew
- **Char Wine**: Smoky alcoholic beverage
- *Culinary Philosophy*: Intense flavors, survival-focused

**Verdant Tangle**:
- **Forest Greens**: Fresh vegetables and herbs
- **Memory Honey**: Honey infused with harmless Echo fragments (gives vivid dreams)
- **Tree Sap Syrup**: Sweet, used in desserts
- **Wild Game**: Hunted from uncorrupted animals
- *Culinary Philosophy*: Natural, organic, minimal processing

**Crystalline Steppes**:
- **Crystal Salt**: Harvested from crystal formations, prized seasoning
- **Nomad Bread**: Flatbread cooked on hot stones
- **Grass-Fed Meat**: From herded animals
- **Prism Tea**: Clear tea that refracts light beautifully
- *Culinary Philosophy*: Simple, portable, communal

**Ironcoast Federation**:
- **Processed Foods**: Convenience over quality
- **Synthesized Nutrients**: Resonance-grown food (controversial)
- **Import Cuisine**: Foods from all regions (for wealthy)
- **Street Food**: Fast, cheap, diverse
- *Culinary Philosophy*: Efficiency, variety, class-divided

#### Cooking System (Game Mechanic)

Players can cook meals at camps using ingredients:

**Example Recipes**:

1. **Traveler's Stew** (Basic)
   - Ingredients: Any meat, any vegetable, water
   - Effect: +50 HP, +20 EE
   - Anyone can make this

2. **Ember-Spiced Roast** (Cindral)
   - Ingredients: Red meat, ember fruit, ash salt
   - Effect: +15% Fire resistance for 3 battles
   - Requires recipe from Emberhearth

3. **Memory Honey Cakes** (Verdant)
   - Ingredients: Forest honey, grain, memory honey
   - Effect: +20% EE regeneration for 2 battles
   - Requires recipe from Roothollow

4. **Crystal-Seasoned Steak** (Steppes)
   - Ingredients: Premium meat, crystal salt, herbs
   - Effect: +10% all stats for 3 battles
   - Requires recipe from Prismfall

5. **Resonance Ration** (Ironcoast)
   - Ingredients: Synthesized protein, energy gel
   - Effect: Full HP/EE restore but -10% stats for 1 battle
   - Available in Gearhaven

6. **Kael's Surprise** (Special)
   - Ingredients: One of each regional specialty
   - Effect: Random powerful buff
   - Unlocked through cooking experimentation

### Religion & Spirituality

#### Pre-Severance

**Resonance Transcendentalism**:
- Belief that Convergence was path to godhood
- Technology as salvation
- Mostly defunct now

**Traditional Faiths**:
- Various religions existed
- Declined as Resonance tech grew
- Some temples still stand, empty

#### Post-Severance

**The Shepherds of Silence** (Most organized):
- All Resonance is sin
- The Severance was divine punishment
- Purity through rejection of crystals
- Prophet Null as spiritual leader
- Ceremonies: Purification rituals, crystal burning
- Sacred text: "The Silence Speaks"

**Free Resonant Spirituality** (Decentralized):
- Balance between technology and nature
- Crystals are neither good nor evil
- Collective wisdom over dogma
- Meditation using safe Resonance
- Ceremonies: Harmony circles, echo remembrance

**Ash Walker Traditions** (Cindral Wastes):
- Fire and ash as sacred
- Ancestor worship through Echoes
- Trials of endurance
- Respect for volcanic forces
- Ceremonies: Ember passing, ash reading

**Corporate Rationalism** (Ironcoast):
- Rejection of religion
- Science and profit as highest values
- Seen as cold by others
- "Faith in progress"

**Personal Spirituality**:
- Most people blend traditions
- Private practices
- Echo shrines in homes
- Prayer to lost loved ones

### Communication & Travel

#### Long Distance Communication

**Crystal Transmitters**:
- Expensive, limited range
- Maintained by specialists
- Available in major cities
- Used for urgent messages

**Couriers**:
- Most common method
- Dangerous but reliable
- Guild system
- Can carry physical items

**Echo Messages**:
- Experimental, rare
- Record message in Echo crystal
- Recipient "experiences" message
- Intimate but requires skill

#### Travel

**On Foot**:
- Most common
- Dangerous (wildlife, bandits, echoes)
- Caravans offer safety in numbers

**Mounts**:
- Horses, camels (steppes)
- Expensive to maintain
- Status symbol

**Vehicles** (Ironcoast mainly):
- Resonance-powered vehicles
- Expensive fuel
- Unreliable outside cities
- Party acquires one mid-game

**Airships** (Rare):
- Initiative monopoly
- Extremely expensive
- Fast but conspicuous

### Death & Burial

#### Traditions

**Echo Preservation**:
- Some preserve loved ones as Echoes
- Controversial (is it them or just memory?)
- Expensive
- Can go wrong (Echo corruption)

**Traditional Burial**:
- Returned to earth
- Ceremonies vary by region
- Memorials with stories

**Crystal Entombment**:
- Wealthy option
- Body partially crystallized
- Permanent preservation
- Seen as vain by some

**Ash Walker Tradition**:
- Cremation in volcanic heat
- Ashes scattered in sacred sites
- Echo of person released to Hollows

**Initiative Protocol**:
- Efficient cremation
- Records kept digitally
- No ceremony (seen as wasteful)

#### The Hollow Plague

When someone overuses Resonance:
- Lose personality, become "Hollow"
- Living but empty
- No known cure (until Sienna's research)
- Families face: keep hoping or mercy kill?
- Major ethical debate

### Superstitions & Folk Beliefs

**Common Beliefs**:

1. **Never look directly at a forming Echo** - Might trap your soul
2. **Crystalline plants mean memory saturation** - Area had many deaths
3. **Echoes are restless dead** - Need to be released or laid to rest
4. **Hollows victims can spread curse** - Avoid touch (not actually true)
5. **Ash protects against corruption** - Cindral tradition (some truth)
6. **Green crystals are safe, red dangerous** - Oversimplified but useful
7. **Full moon strengthens Echoes** - Poetic, not proven
8. **The Convergence is still alive** - Conspiracy theory (that's true)

**Regional Superstitions**:

**Cindral**:
- Volcanic eruptions are angry spirits
- First born must walk through ash for luck

**Verdant**:
- Trees remember everything
- Don't cut living crystal plants (bad luck)

**Steppes**:
- Trading brings good fortune
- Never turn away travelers (they might be Echoes)

**Ironcoast**:
- Seeing superboss Echoes is good luck (wishful thinking)
- Initiative workers: Don't question orders (survival)

## Language & Communication

### Common Tongue

**Universal Language**:
- Evolved from pre-Severance global language
- Everyone speaks it
- Regional dialects and accents

### Echo Language (Emerging)

**Ash's Innovation**:
- Pure emotional communication
- Transcends words
- Can convey complex concepts
- Requires Resonance sensitivity
- May be future of communication

### Technical Jargon

**Resonance Tech Terms**:
- Tuning, harmonic, frequency, overflow
- Echo, fragment, manifestation
- Hollow, corruption, purification
- Convergence, severance, anchor

### Slang by Region

**Ironcoast**:
- "Crystal clear" = understood
- "Hollow" = emotionless person (insult)
- "Echoing" = repeating yourself

**Steppes**:
- "Trade fair" = good deal/honest
- "Nomad's path" = uncertain future
- "Crystal blessed" = lucky

**Cindral**:
- "Ash walker" = survivor (compliment)
- "Ember heart" = passionate
- "Cooled" = gave up/defeated

**Verdant**:
- "Overgrown" = complicated
- "Root deep" = traditional
- "Memory bloom" = nostalgia
