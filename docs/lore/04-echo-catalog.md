# Echo Fragment Catalog

## Understanding Echoes

Echoes are crystallized moments of consciousness—frozen emotions, memories, and experiences from before and after The Severance. They range from mundane daily moments to world-changing events. Each Echo tells a story.

---

## Echo Categories

### Combat Echoes
Used in battle for tactical advantages. Recharge after each fight.

### Tuning Echoes
Used to customize equipment with special properties.

### Story Echoes
Pure narrative content that reveals world lore when viewed.

### Companion Echoes
Unlock character backstory and bonding events.

## Paradox & Glitch Echoes

These Echoes are born from the collapse of linear time or the corruption of the Resonance network. They are unpredictable and often "wrong" in a way that provides immense power at a cost.

### Paradox Tier (Black/White Flicker)

**"The Un-Memory"**
- **Type**: Unique
- **Effect**: Erases the last 2 turns of combat for both sides. All HP, EE, and status are reverted to their state 2 turns ago.
- **Memory**: A memory of an event that was prevented from happening.
- **Found**: Center of the Hollows, only during an Un-Sun.

**"Future Self's Advice"**
- **Type**: Support
- **Effect**: Guarantees the next 3 attacks will be Critical Hits. 
- **Memory**: An older version of the user speaking to their current self.
- **Found**: Near Spatial Folds in the Ironcoast.

**"The Road Not Taken"**
- **Type**: Unique
- **Effect**: Swap the party's current HP and EE percentages.
- **Memory**: A version of the party that chose a different profession.
- **Found**: Hidden in the "What-If" archives of Prismfall.

### Glitch Tier (Static Rainbow)

**"No-Clip"**
- **Type**: Support
- **Effect**: Party becomes immune to all physical damage for 3 turns, but cannot use physical attacks.
- **Memory**: A moment when the laws of physics failed during a Static Storm.
- **Found**: Caught in the middle of a Static Storm.

**"Recursive Punch"**
- **Type**: Attack
- **Effect**: Deals moderate damage. If the enemy isn't defeated, the attack repeats itself for 0 cost next turn, increasing in power by 20%. This continues until the loop is broken (enemy dies or user misses).
- **Memory**: A Recursive Echo of a bar fight.
- **Found**: Roothollow, near a perpetually slamming door.

**"Corrupted Data"**
- **Type**: Debuff
- **Effect**: Randomizes the enemy's elemental weaknesses every turn.
- **Memory**: A file from a Pre-Severance server that was partially deleted.
- **Found**: Deep within the ruins of Gearhaven's mainframe.

## Echo Hunting Mini-Game

### The Hunt

When Kael detects an Echo in the environment:

1. **Detection Phase**: Echo appears as shimmering distortion
2. **Approach Phase**: Get close without startling it (some Echoes flee)
3. **Resonance Phase**: Match your Resonance frequency to the Echo
4. **Capture Phase**: Mini-game (varies by Echo type)

### Capture Mini-Games

**Harmonic Matching**: Match tones to stabilize Echo
**Memory Puzzle**: Piece together fragmented images
**Emotional Tuning**: Select correct emotional response
**Time Trial**: Capture before Echo fades

### Echo Behavior Types

**Passive**: Doesn't move, easy to collect
**Fleeing**: Runs away if approached carelessly
**Aggressive**: Attacks if you fail capture
**Conditional**: Only appears under specific conditions
**Timed**: Appears only at certain times or weather

## Echo Display & Collection

### Journal System

Collected Echoes appear in Kael's journal with:
- Visual representation (crystalline memory fragment)
- Name and classification
- Memory content (text/imagery)
- Lore revealed
- Mechanical effects (if combat Echo)
- Collection location and date
- Personal notes from Kael (flavor text)

### Collection Rewards

- **25% Complete**: Unlock Echo tuning at blacksmiths
- **50% Complete**: Kael gains "Echo Mastery" passive ability
- **75% Complete**: Access to secret Echo trader
- **100% Complete**: Ultimate Echo weapon for Kael
- **All Unique Echoes**: Secret ending variation

## Echo Philosophy

Echoes aren't just gameplay mechanics—they're the game's soul. Each one tells a story about:
- What was lost in The Severance
- What humanity valued
- Individual human experiences
- The cost of progress
- The beauty in ordinary moments

Players who engage deeply with Echo collection get the richest narrative experience, piecing together the world's history through fragments of countless lives.

*"We are all echoes of those who came before. The question is: what echo will you leave behind?"* —Kael's Journal
