# Time Fantasy Style Guide

## Core Principles
- **Grid**: 16x16 pixel base.
- **SNES Aesthetic**: Earthy tones, high contrast, clean outlines.
- **Proportions**: Characters are 2-3 heads tall.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Palette**: Avoid pure black (#000000) and pure white (#FFFFFF).
- **Shadows**: Use #354049 (dark blue-gray).
- **Highlights**: Use #ECFEFC (pale blue) or #FBFBE8 (pale yellow).
- **Darks**: Shift towards purple/blue and lose saturation.
- **Lights**: Shift towards yellow or cyan.

## Asset Formats
### Character Walk Sheets
- **Dimensions**: 48x64 (for 16x16 frames) or 144x192 (for 48x48 frames).
- **Layout**: 3 columns (walk1, idle, walk2) x 4 rows (Down, Left, Right, Up).

### Tilesets
- **Terrain (A5)**: 8x16 grid of 16x16 tiles. Use single-tile fill for ground.
- **Objects (B)**: 16x16 grid of 16x16 tiles. Contains multi-tile objects like trees and houses.

### Portraits
- **Size**: 32x32, 48x48, or 64x64 depending on UI space.
